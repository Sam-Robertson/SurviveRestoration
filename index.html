You are a young Joseph Smith working on the farm with your Dad. Money is tight, but you manage. A neighbor offers to pay you to look for burried treasure on his property. Do you go?
Instructions: Your goal is to continue the restoration of the Gospel of Jesus Christ without falling into many of the endless pitfalls that faces the early saints, the prophet, and those who surrounded them. Based on your doctrinal understanding and knowledge you should be able to maintain survivable stats. The Public Opinion, Saint Moral, and Financial Stablity stats have five levels: Excellent, Good, Decent, Poor, Very Poor, Terrible. If any or your levels reach terrible then you lose the game. The levels are based on different stages of the early church: The Beginnings (1820 -1831), Two Church Centers (1831-1838), Nauvoo (1839 -1846), and Chruch in the West (1846-1950). However, the main focus is on the early church, so the first levels will have more questions and more pitfals that will hinder the chruch. Good Luck!
